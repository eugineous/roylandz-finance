All-in-one money management

Roylandz Finance combines budgeting, saving, forecasting, and business support into one intuitive workspace.

Personal budgeting

Track income from salary, side gigs, and freelance jobs. Log expenses in clear categories and compare planned vs. actual spending in real time.

Savings & goal tracking

Create goals like “Trip to Mombasa” or “Emergency Fund.” Allocate income towards each target and celebrate milestones with motivating badges.

Cash flow planning

Plot recurring bills on a calendar and forecast daily balances. Spot heavy-spend weeks ahead of time to avoid running out of cash.

Side hustle & business support

Separate personal and business budgets, toggle between contexts, and view a simple profit-and-loss snapshot for your hustle.

Reports & summaries

Visualize spending trends with export-ready charts. Compare months, spot overspending, and share summaries with partners or advisors.

Fast, friendly onboarding

Start with a guided tour, sample data, or guest mode. Add income, budget categories, and goals in minutes to personalize your dashboard.

Built for every money journey

Roylandz adapts to students, young professionals, hustlers, families, and small business owners.

Students & youth

Stretch allowances or campus income. Visual feedback teaches budgeting habits and boosts savings rates for the next semester.

Young professionals

Balance rent, transport, loan repayments, and future goals. Forecast payday vs. bill dates to avoid end-of-month crunch.

Hustlers & freelancers

Track irregular income source by source. Let Roylandz suggest how much to save during busy weeks to cushion quieter periods.

Families & household managers

Plan shared budgets, set family goals, and review monthly reports together for transparent money conversations.

Small business owners

Manage shop finances alongside personal budgets. Keep funds separate yet connected and understand profitability at a glance.

The story behind Roylandz

Eugine Micah built Roylandz Finance after years of highlighting youth voices across Kenya as a journalist, presenter, and digital strategist. From Kisumu and Kakamega to Nairobi’s media stages, he witnessed how financial stress holds back students, creatives, and hustlers.

Roylandz Media started to tell authentic African stories with world-class production. Roylandz Finance extends that mission into personal finance: delivering world-class tools with local authenticity so every user can grow with confidence.

“Managing money should feel simple, empowering, and culturally aware. Roylandz is the companion I wished we all had.” — Eugine Micah, Founder
